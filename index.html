MFEM is a free, lightweight, scalable C++ library for finite element methods.

Features

  • Arbitrary high-order finite element meshes and spaces.
  • Wide variety of finite element discretization approaches.
  • Conforming and nonconforming adaptive mesh refinement.
  • Scalable from laptops to GPU-accelerated supercomputers.
  • ... and many more.

MFEM is used in many projects, including BLAST, RF-SciDAC, VisIt, Cardioid, xSDK, SciDAC/FASTMath, and the Center for Efficient Exascale Discretizations in the Exascale Computing Project.

See also our Gallery, Publications and News pages.
